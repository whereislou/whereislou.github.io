Methodology & Caveats

This analysis uses a difference-in-differences (DiD) approach comparing Amazon revenue in treatment states (receiving incremental Meta ad spend since Mar 17) against control states (business as usual).

  • Pre-period: Feb 22 – Mar 16, 2026 (23 days) · Post-period: Mar 17 – Apr 09, 2026 (24 days)
  • Statistical tests: Welch's t-test (p = 0.026) and Mann-Whitney U (p = 0.051) on state-level % changes
  • Bootstrap: 10,000 resamples → 95% CI [+1.4pp, +15.0pp], 99.4% positive
  • Parallel trends assumption: Check the weekly index chart — groups should track closely pre-test
  • Excluded: California, Alaska, Hawaii, territories, and non-US orders
  • Note: Pre-period is 23 days vs 24-day post-period (1-day mismatch due to test start timing)
  • Limitations: Short post-period; seasonal patterns may differ by state; Amazon attribution to Meta is indirect (halo effect); small states have noisy signals
