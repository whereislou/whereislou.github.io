Methodology

  • Pre-Period: February 10 – March 17, 2026 (36 days)
  • Post-Period: March 18 – April 22, 2026 (36 days)
  • Treatment: 24 states with increased Meta spend
  • Control: 23 states at business-as-usual
  • Excluded: CA, AK, HI
  • Shopify Source: Matrixify export (paid orders, US, shipping province)
  • Amazon Source: NetSuite export (USD revenue by state)
  • New Customer: Shopify Customer Orders Count = 1
  • Statistical Tests: Welch’s t-test (primary) + Mann-Whitney U (robustness) on state-level % changes
  • Confidence Intervals: Bootstrap percentile method, 10,000 resamples, 95% level
